What changed in the "After" panel: (1) Unified header treatment — "Why this risk applies" / "How it's controlled" now use the same small-caps gray style as their sub-heads instead of large blue underlined text. Section > subsection hierarchy comes from position and size, not color. (2) Indentation — content within a section sits indented 14px from the left edge of the section header. Makes the grouping visible. (3) No count-pill badges in sub-heads — the "1 High" / "1 Class C" chips next to IAG Issues and OREs are gone. The severity pill on the table row communicates the same thing. (4) Consistent ID styling — all IDs (IT-102, ARA-xxxx, F-1001, ORE-003) use the same muted monospace chip. Sub-risk names are now dark prose, not blue. (5) ARA ID chips sit next to their signal body instead of floating to the far right (no more margin-left: auto on .signal-ids).
